About Me

I've been a Alto Saxophone player for 3 years in band classes. The highest ranked class I got to was Middle School Symphonic Band. I was also in Marching Band for half a season. My mother and cousin were also Alto Saxophone players and were both in Marching Band. I hope I am able to teach you what I know and more, as my YouTube Channel is open to any Alto Saxophone players.
