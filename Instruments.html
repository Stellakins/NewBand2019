Flute

The Flute is a woodwind instrument, even though it's body is made of metal. The assembly is very easy for this instrument. Some video's will be provided on the assembly and scales. You want to try to hold the Flute at a 90 degree angle the best you can. Some band directors get very picky about the angle your holding your Flute. The Flute is small and light weight, so it is really easy to hold. Flutes usually have melody, as their range is very high. You must have good wind support for the higher notes.
